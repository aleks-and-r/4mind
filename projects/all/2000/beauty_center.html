The interior of the beauty salon "Bulevar" is an open space. The idea is that the circular manicure table is the main motif of the interior, connecting both beauticians and customers during the service into one compact whole. In addition to its central position, this part of the interior is accentuated by a light ceiling and represents the basic communication point. The materialization of the salon is in contrast: form cold and dark granite floor to terracotta red walls.
